About Us

Founded in 2017 in the quaint Karoo town of Cradock, our company is dedicated to delivering exceptional service tailored to each client's needs. Whether it's repairing a minor leak or transforming your outdoor space with a premium fibreglass swimming pool, we handle it all.

As accredited installers for Pools for Africa, we collaborate closely with leading suppliers to ensure top-quality products and outstanding service at every step.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do fiberglass installations?

Yes, we are accredited installers for Pools for Africa and specialize in high-quality fiberglass pool installations.

Where are you located?

We're based in Cradock, Eastern Cape, but also serve surrounding areas

What types of pool services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of pool services including:

  • Fiberglass pool installation
  • Swimming pool construction
  • Swimming pool revamps
  • Pumps and filters installation and repair
  • Farm dam conversions
  • Pool regrouting and tiling
How long does a typical pool installation take?

The timeline varies depending on the project complexity, but most fiberglass pool installations are completed within 2-4 weeks

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive pool maintenance services to keep your pool in perfect condition year-round.
